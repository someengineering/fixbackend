What is CSPM and why do I need it?

In the era of cloud computing, the scale and complexity of cloud resources have escalated dramatically. With anywhere from a handful to potentially hundreds or even thousands of engineers deploying, modifying, and managing cloud resources across multiple platforms, the landscape of a company's cloud infrastructure has become vast and intricate. This expansion means no single person has full visibility into every asset or configuration within the infrastructure. It's within this context that Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM) becomes indispensable.

Security score summary of all accounts

CSPM tools like Fix are designed to provide a panoramic view of an organization's cloud security posture, automating the detection of risks that can stem from:

  • Misconfigurations: As cloud environments grow, so does the likelihood of misconfigurations. Simple oversights in settings can lead to significant vulnerabilities, such as exposed data stores or improperly secured compute instances and databases.
  • Compliance Drifts: With regulations evolving and varying across jurisdictions, maintaining compliance in the cloud becomes a moving target. CSPM ensures that your infrastructure adheres to the latest standards, protecting against penalties and breaches.
  • Orphaned Resources: Among the most insidious risks are the forgotten, unused, or "orphaned" resources that accumulate over time, such as volume snapshots of sensitive data (e.g., customer databases) or unused virtual machines. These abandoned assets can not only incur unnecessary costs but also pose significant security risks if left unmonitored and unprotected.

Given the dynamic nature of cloud resources, with new services being spun up and down daily, CSPM serves as an essential system to ensure that every piece of your cloud puzzle is accounted for, secure, and compliant. Fix leverages advanced CSPM capabilities to scan and identify potential vulnerabilities across your entire cloud ecosystem, ensuring not just security, but peace of mind in a landscape where traditional oversight methods no longer suffice.
