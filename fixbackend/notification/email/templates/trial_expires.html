Easy Steps to get a Fix subscription

  1. Workspace Settings: Login to Fix and locate the Workspace Settings icon at the bottom left of your screen.
  2. Billing: Within the Workspace Settings, you'll find the "Billing" section. Here's where can select a subscription plan that suits your needs.
  3. Select the plan and add the payment method: Follow the instructions on the screen to select a plan and add your payment method.

What Comes Next?

If you won't get a subscription, soon after your trial expires your account will downgraded to the Free plan. Only a single user will be able to log in, and the data we collected about your cloud accounts will no longer be available in Fix. You will also lose access to the features available in the paid plans.

Here for You

If any step in the process feels unclear, or if you encounter any bumps along the road, our team is standing by. Contact us at support@fix.security or ping us on Discord.
